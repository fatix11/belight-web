Who We Are

To be light for the community

BeLight Pro Communitas (BPC) is a local non-governmental organization established in 2024 and registered under the FDRE Authority for Civil Society Organizations (ACSO).

Founded by young, passionate, and socially responsible Ethiopians, BPC was born out of a profound desire to create meaningful and lasting change in their community. Their shared vision of an equitable society led to the formation of the organization.

Rooted in compassion and faith-based commitment, BPC strives to empower vulnerable communities — especially disadvantaged women and children — through community-based interventions that address immediate needs while fostering long-term resilience, dignity, and self-reliance.

Our Vision

Foster a future where underprivileged individuals and communities are equipped with the resources and opportunities needed to build sustainable and dignified lives.

Our Mission

To enhance the well-being and resilience of deprived communities through inclusive development initiatives in WASH, Education, Livelihood, and social support.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Faith-Based Commitment

We are driven by faith principles to serve those in greatest need, bringing light where there is darkness.

Compassion & Service

We operate with empathy and deep respect for all beneficiaries, honoring the dignity of every person we serve.

Community-Centered

We recognize and empower communities as active agents and leaders in their own change — not passive recipients.

Collaboration & Partnership

We work together with others on shared values to maximize impact and reach more communities effectively.

Sustainability

We ensure long-term, continuous mission fulfillment — building systems that outlast our direct involvement.

Transparency & Accountability

We uphold the highest standards of ethical conduct, financial integrity, and zero tolerance for fraud or exploitation.

Where We Work

Target Areas

In its initial phase, BPC concentrates its interventions in selected priority areas, with a focus on communities affected by conflict, displacement, and socio-economic vulnerability.
